### 關鍵觀點

- **資料結構與演算法是基礎**：面試包含兩輪資料結構與演算法測試。例如，第一輪要求解決「最長不重複子字串」問題，採用滑動視窗法，時間與空間複雜度皆為 O(n)；以及「航班路線連接」問題，使用圖形遍歷（DFS 或 BFS）解決，顯示對效率的理解至關重要。[\[1\]](https://app.heptabase.com/f3c4fc0d-fe07-4b10-932c-6f968c1f5ab0/card/01021294-bc79-43ca-86ef-5163f36debb1?pdfPage=1&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A615.315%2C%22top%22%3A1281.6%2C%22width%22%3A683.895%2C%22height%22%3A57.600000000000136%7D)[\[2\]](https://app.heptabase.com/f3c4fc0d-fe07-4b10-932c-6f968c1f5ab0/card/01021294-bc79-43ca-86ef-5163f36debb1?pdfPage=1&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A598.17%2C%22top%22%3A1440%2C%22width%22%3A293.3700000000001%2C%22height%22%3A72%7D)[\[3\]](https://app.heptabase.com/f3c4fc0d-fe07-4b10-932c-6f968c1f5ab0/card/01021294-bc79-43ca-86ef-5163f36debb1?pdfPage=1&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A607.695%2C%22top%22%3A1612.8%2C%22width%22%3A678.18%2C%22height%22%3A86.39999999999986%7D)[\[21\]](https://app.heptabase.com/f3c4fc0d-fe07-4b10-932c-6f968c1f5ab0/card/01021294-bc79-43ca-86ef-5163f36debb1?pdfPage=1&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A594.36%2C%22top%22%3A2232%2C%22width%22%3A358.14%2C%22height%22%3A144%7D)[\[4\]](https://app.heptabase.com/f3c4fc0d-fe07-4b10-932c-6f968c1f5ab0/card/01021294-bc79-43ca-86ef-5163f36debb1?pdfPage=1&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A594.36%2C%22top%22%3A2534.3999999999996%2C%22width%22%3A300.9899999999999%2C%22height%22%3A72.00000000000045%7D)[\[5\]](https://app.heptabase.com/f3c4fc0d-fe07-4b10-932c-6f968c1f5ab0/card/01021294-bc79-43ca-86ef-5163f36debb1?pdfPage=1&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A598.17%2C%22top%22%3A2678.4%2C%22width%22%3A506.7299999999999%2C%22height%22%3A100.80000000000018%7D)

- **精通 Swift 並行處理**：Swift 5.5 引入的 `async/await` 結構化並行機制是核心考點。底層採用 GCD 的協作式執行緒池排程技術，任務運行於輕量級的「continuations」之上，並且 `Task {}` 和 `TaskGroup` 用於管理並行任務。[\[9\]](https://app.heptabase.com/f3c4fc0d-fe07-4b10-932c-6f968c1f5ab0/card/01021294-bc79-43ca-86ef-5163f36debb1?pdfPage=1&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A598.17%2C%22top%22%3A4564.8%2C%22width%22%3A470.5350000000002%2C%22height%22%3A86.39999999999964%7D)[\[10\]](https://app.heptabase.com/f3c4fc0d-fe07-4b10-932c-6f968c1f5ab0/card/01021294-bc79-43ca-86ef-5163f36debb1?pdfPage=1&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A611.505%2C%22top%22%3A4694.400000000001%2C%22width%22%3A563.88%2C%22height%22%3A172.80000000000018%7D)

- **系統設計能力不可或缺**：對於大型應用程式，面試官特別重視擴展性與清晰的架構。理想的解決方案應包含 MVVM+C 架構、使用 URLSession/Combine 進行網路連線、CoreData 或 SQLite 實現離線模式、Keychain 儲存用戶數據以確保安全性，以及模組化架構來處理不同功能。[\[13\]](https://app.heptabase.com/f3c4fc0d-fe07-4b10-932c-6f968c1f5ab0/card/01021294-bc79-43ca-86ef-5163f36debb1?pdfPage=1&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A611.505%2C%22top%22%3A6393.6%2C%22width%22%3A432.43500000000006%2C%22height%22%3A72%7D)[\[14\]](https://app.heptabase.com/f3c4fc0d-fe07-4b10-932c-6f968c1f5ab0/card/01021294-bc79-43ca-86ef-5163f36debb1?pdfPage=1&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A603.885%2C%22top%22%3A7200%2C%22width%22%3A348.615%2C%22height%22%3A43.19999999999982%7D)

- **深入理解 Swift 語言特性**：面試會探究 Swift 的進階概念，例如將類別標記為 `final` 的影響、Actor 如何透過隔離可變狀態來確保執行緒安全，以及 `struct`、`class` 和 `actor` 之間在值類型、引用類型和並行安全方面的本質區別。[\[15\]](https://app.heptabase.com/f3c4fc0d-fe07-4b10-932c-6f968c1f5ab0/card/01021294-bc79-43ca-86ef-5163f36debb1?pdfPage=1&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A603.885%2C%22top%22%3A7329.6%2C%22width%22%3A373.38%2C%22height%22%3A43.19999999999982%7D)[\[16\]](https://app.heptabase.com/f3c4fc0d-fe07-4b10-932c-6f968c1f5ab0/card/01021294-bc79-43ca-86ef-5163f36debb1?pdfPage=1&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A643.89%2C%22top%22%3A7876.8%2C%22width%22%3A354.33000000000004%2C%22height%22%3A115.20000000000073%7D)

- **展現實際問題解決能力**：除了理論知識，文章強調應徵者需能展現離線模式、緩存機制、擴展性方案等實際應用能力。例如，在系統設計中考慮到推播通知、報到流程、API 呼叫及本地儲存登機證，這些都是大型航空應用程式的實際需求。[\[13\]](https://app.heptabase.com/f3c4fc0d-fe07-4b10-932c-6f968c1f5ab0/card/01021294-bc79-43ca-86ef-5163f36debb1?pdfPage=1&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A611.505%2C%22top%22%3A6393.6%2C%22width%22%3A432.43500000000006%2C%22height%22%3A72%7D)[\[22\]](https://app.heptabase.com/f3c4fc0d-fe07-4b10-932c-6f968c1f5ab0/card/01021294-bc79-43ca-86ef-5163f36debb1?pdfPage=1&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A607.695%2C%22top%22%3A6696%2C%22width%22%3A691.515%2C%22height%22%3A115.19999999999982%7D)[\[20\]](https://app.heptabase.com/f3c4fc0d-fe07-4b10-932c-6f968c1f5ab0/card/01021294-bc79-43ca-86ef-5163f36debb1?pdfPage=1&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A607.695%2C%22top%22%3A9806.400000000001%2C%22width%22%3A489.5849999999999%2C%22height%22%3A57.599999999998545%7D)

# Note

Drizzled: A culinary term referring to the technique of lightly pouring a liquid, often a sauce or dressing, over food in a controlled manner. This method is commonly used to enhance the presentation and flavor of dishes, allowing for even distribution of flavors while maintaining the visual appeal of the food. Drizzling is often employed with various liquids, such as oils, vinaigrettes, syrups, or sauces, and can be executed using tools like a squeeze bottle or spoon to achieve a desired effect.
